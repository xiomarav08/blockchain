Blockchain reforestation system

The system aims to plant forest trees (Abarco, Moncoro, Caoba) and Cocoa, through a platform that integrates blockchain technology through a smart contract, in order to foster the care of our planet and help small producers of the Echar Pa Lante program initially in the department of Caldas - Colombia with a traceable and transparent technology

A.Consideration

The system integrates two types of purchase, depending on the number of trees that the customer wishes to plant. These purchases are stipulated as follows: 1) Forest Sowing: • Purchases over 1,000 trees: $ 2.75 • Purchases of less than 1,000 trees: $ 3.0 • Purchases greater than or equal to 10,000 trees: $ 1.75 2) Cocoa Planting: The customer can buy a cocoa tree for a small producer and his family. In this case the value of each tree will be $ 1.75. Only the respective sowing will be made to the producer selected by Terra Forza S.A.S when an estimated 50 purchases are made

B.Method of payment

The trees will be purchased through the Pay Pal platform and these purchases will be associated with a collection account of Terra Forza S.A.S.

C.Obligations Terra Forza S.A.S

The obligations below refer to the two types of purchases that make up the system: 1) Obligations for forest planting: • Select from the nursery the seedling (s) to be sown. • Georeference the planting site of the seedling (s). • Move and sow the seedling (s). • Fertilize the seedling (s) at the time of planting. • Perform cultural tasks to maintain the plant (s) during the first two years of life while it is being strengthened. • Reseed the plant (s), if it dies within two years of maintenance due to factors external to it. 2) Obligations for sowing Cocoa: • Select and graft the cocoa seedling (s). • Move the seedling (s) to the farm of the selected small producer. • Sign with the small producer a commitment to plant and care for the seedling (s). • Sow and georeference the seedling (s). • Follow up the small producer during the first year, monitoring the development of the seedling (s).

D. Duration of agreements

The duration of each obligation is stipulated according to the planting obligations described above. The signing of the contract between both parties is unmodifiable and unique for each purchase through a smart contract.
